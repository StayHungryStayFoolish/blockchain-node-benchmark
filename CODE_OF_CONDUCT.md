# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, practical, and welcoming for contributors
with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep technical disagreements focused on evidence and tradeoffs.
- Assume good intent, but be clear when something is unsafe or incorrect.
- Do not publish secrets, private RPC endpoints, customer data, or private
  infrastructure details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, abusive automation, or intentionally misleading reports.
- Attempts to bypass security, branch protection, or disclosure processes.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the repository maintainer through GitHub. Report
security vulnerabilities through the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, restrict participation, or take
other action needed to keep the project healthy.
